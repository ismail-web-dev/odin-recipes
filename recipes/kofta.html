Kofta Kebabs

Description

These Middle Eastern kebabs are made with ground beef and lamb, mixed with fresh parsley, onions, garlic, and warm spices, and then grilled to perfection. Serve them with pita bread, a simple tomato salad, and a side of tzatziki for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, ground lamb, onion, parsley, garlic, cinnamon, allspice, cumin, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt until well combined.
  2. Divide the mixture into 16 portions and shape each portion into a cylinder around a skewer.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill or grill pan to high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  4. Grill the kebabs, turning occasionally, until browned and cooked through, about 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the kebabs to a serving platter and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.
