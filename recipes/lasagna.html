lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. This recipe is sure to be a hit with your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) spaghetti sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 6 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink; drain.
  2. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1-1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and parsley.
  4. Spoon a third of the meat sauce into a greased 8-in. square baking dish.
  5. Layer with three noodles, half of the cheese mixture, and a third of the meat sauce.
  6. Repeat layers of noodles, cheese mixture, and meat sauce. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover and bake at 350Â° for 45 minutes. Uncover; bake for 10 minutes longer or until bubbly. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
